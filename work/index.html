Selected Work

OpenImageIO

Logo and brand guidelines for an open source C++ library.

OpenImageIO, a library for reading and writing images, is used extensively in animation and VFX studios all over the world, and is also incorporated into several commercial products. I created the logo and brand guidelines for the project.

Velocity Lapse

Powerful time lapse app for Android.

Velocity Lapse is an Android app to create time lapse videos. It brings together a clutter-free interface, manually adjustable camera settings, and project-based flexibility into one app.

Flameshot

Modern website for a popular screenshot software.

The Flameshot open source project needed a website to reflect their easy-to-use screenshot tool. I worked closely with the project maintainers to tailor the website to their needs using modern web tech and best practices.

OPC of Ava

New website for a life saving organization.

Options is a Pro-life, faith-based ministry in Ava serving the community for over 15 years. I was excited to work with them, creating a new website and content to make it easier for vistors to find information and get support at their locations.
