A bit about me and my work

Hey there!

I’m a freelance web designer/developer from the U.S, serving clients with design, development, and tech support services. I am also currently working on a powerful time-lapse app for Android called Velocity Lapse.

I’ve been very blessed to work on a variety of different projects with a range of clients. I love doing meaningful work that helps people and glorifies Yahweh, our Heavenly Father.

Creating things, collaborating with others, and learning new tech drives me to create and contribute to open-source software projects, most notably Gimel Studio and Bible Notify.

Feel free to contact me with any questions or comments you have. Thank you.
